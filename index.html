🌍 Planning of the Plantation Drives

Tree plantation is one of the most effective and sustainable methods of protecting the environment. Trees help reduce pollution, improve biodiversity, prevent soil erosion, conserve water resources, and combat climate change.

This CSP focuses on planning plantation drives and creating environmental awareness among the people of **Kotha Peta, Gooty**.

📖 About The Project

This CSP promotes practical climate action through organized plantation planning. This Community Service Project was conducted in **Kotha Peta, Gooty, Ananthapuram District** as part of the academic curriculum of the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering, GATES Institute of Technology.

The project focuses on understanding the importance of tree plantation, studying plantation planning techniques, creating environmental awareness, and encouraging community participation in plantation activities.

🎯 Project Objectives

Clear goals for a greener Kotha Peta

🌿 Awareness

Promote environmental awareness among village residents.

👥 Participation

Encourage community participation in plantation activities.

📚 Techniques

Study proper plantation techniques and care practices.

🌱 Nursery

Understand nursery management for healthy saplings.

💚 Greenery

Improve village greenery and public spaces.

💨 Pollution

Reduce environmental pollution through green cover.

🛠️ Plantation Techniques

01. Site Selection
Choose open, safe, accessible areas
02. Soil Assessment
Study texture, drainage, fertility
03. Land Preparation
Clear waste, mark locations
04. Pit Digging
Dig pits of proper size
05. Seedling Selection
Select healthy native saplings
06. Plantation
Place carefully, water immediately
07. Watering
Scheduled watering, mulch
08. Protection
Install guards, protect saplings
09. Monitoring
Check growth regularly
